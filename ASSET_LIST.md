# Taqueria Los Hermanos — Complete Website Asset List

Companion to `DESIGN_SPEC.md`. Every asset includes purpose, dimensions, style direction, and an AI image prompt.

> **⚠ Real vs. AI policy (important):** The research is blunt — 84% of guests look at photos before ordering, and inauthentic/stock-looking photography destroys trust. Assets marked **[REAL — AI placeholder only]** must come from the three planned shoots (menu, brothers/kitchen, catering event) before launch; their AI prompts exist so design mockups and the build aren't blocked waiting on photography. Assets marked **[AI-OK]** (textures, illustrations, icons, brand graphics) can ship as generated/vector art.

**Global style constants** (use in every AI prompt for consistency):
- Palette: warm cream `#FAF3E7`, terra cotta `#C8552D`, deep adobo red `#8C2B1E`, charcoal `#2B2420`, gold `#D9A441`
- Photo look: natural window light, warm tones, shallow depth of field, real imperfect surfaces (worn wood, talavera tile, handmade tortillas), no clinical studio white
- Illustration look: hand-painted gouache/sign-painter style, visible brush texture, mercado hand-lettering heritage

**Format/perf rules:** photos exported AVIF + WebP fallback, ≤200KB above-fold / ≤120KB below-fold; icons and illustrations as SVG; all dimensions below are @2x retina masters — serve responsive sizes from them.
